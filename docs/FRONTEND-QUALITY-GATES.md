# AKANIL Front-End Quality Gates

A section or page is not complete until it passes all applicable gates.

## QG-01 — Institutional clarity

- Akanil ownership is visible.
- Gateway, Forum and future chamber are not conflated.
- The page does not look like a generic event website or marketplace.

## QG-02 — Stakeholder value

- A Moroccan institutional or business stakeholder understands why the Gateway matters within the first viewport and the next two sections.
- Claims are tied to capability, process or evidence.
- Calls to action are specific and non-promissory.

## QG-03 — Visual authorship

- The composition shows editorial judgement, not template assembly.
- AI-generated concept art is used as atmosphere or illustration, not as the entire visual system.
- Semantic HTML text is never replaced by text baked into generated images.

## QG-04 — Interaction quality

- Motion explains hierarchy, route, process, trust or progression.
- No scroll hijacking.
- No interaction exists only to impress.
- Keyboard, pointer and touch interactions are all supported.

## QG-05 — Trilingual experience

- Arabic is native RTL.
- French and English are independently edited.
- Mixed-direction strings, numbers, acronyms and identifiers display correctly.
- Language switching preserves the current page context.

## QG-06 — Accessibility

- Target WCAG 2.2 Level AA.
- Visible focus, semantic landmarks, labels, alt text and reduced-motion behavior are present.
- The interface does not rely on color alone.

## QG-07 — Performance

Project targets at the 75th percentile:
- LCP <= 2.5 s.
- INP <= 200 ms.
- CLS <= 0.1.

Large motion assets must have poster images, lazy loading and mobile fallbacks.

## QG-08 — Responsive behavior

- Test narrow phone, common phone, tablet, laptop and wide desktop.
- Mobile is an authored experience, not a collapsed desktop layout.
- No clipped Arabic headings, maps, menus or motion canvases.

## QG-09 — Evidence and status

- Conceptual routes and visuals are labelled.
- Target organizations are not shown as confirmed partners.
- Public claims have a status and source path where applicable.

## QG-10 — Engineering handoff

- Components have clear ownership and names.
- Design tokens are centralized.
- Content is separated from presentation.
- Tests, build instructions and asset licenses/statuses are documented.
