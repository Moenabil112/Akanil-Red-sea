# AKANIL Front-End Project Baseline

## Project identity

- Master organization: Akanil for Development and Investment.
- Project: Akanil Morocco–Red Sea Economic Gateway.
- Arabic name: بوابة أكانيل الاقتصادية بين المغرب والبحر الأحمر.
- First activation programme: Morocco–Sudan Economic Forum.
- Future chamber: a separate future legal and institutional pathway; do not present it as an existing entity.

## Governing narrative

The Gateway is a permanent Moroccan business-development, qualification, relationship-management and follow-up infrastructure connecting Morocco, Sudan and the Red Sea economic space.

Akanil is the founder and executive operator. The Forum is the first activation programme, not the whole project.

Morocco and Sudan are value-chain partners. Do not frame Sudan merely as a raw-material source.

The approved shared-value logic is:

Sudanese resource or capability
+ Moroccan processing or expertise
+ finance and logistics
+ regional distribution
= measurable shared value.

## Primary audiences

1. Moroccan public and economic institutions.
2. Moroccan manufacturers, exporters and service providers.
3. Qualified Sudanese business decision-makers.
4. Financial, industrial, logistics, technology and knowledge partners.
5. Akanil internal stakeholders.

## Public-interface status

The interface is an institutional introduction window. It is not yet an operational public portal.

Do not imply that the following capabilities are active unless they are actually implemented:

- user onboarding;
- opportunity submission;
- data rooms;
- automated matching;
- production AI agents;
- payment or contracting;
- public registration;
- live logistics intelligence.

## Authority and trust

- AI proposes; an authorized human reviews and decides.
- Do not confirm a partner, sponsor, institution, route, investment or opportunity without evidence.
- Concept images are not documentary proof.
- Preserve source attribution, opportunity ownership, purpose limitation, consent and least privilege.
