# P3 — Claims, Privacy and Publication Boundaries

Companion to the P0/P1/P2 claims registers. Enforced by
`tests/p3-forum-engagement.test.ts`.

## Public status of the Forum

The Forum is presented as a **proposed qualification and engagement programme —
participation subject to review and invitation**. A status pill carries this on
the hub and programme pages, and the programme is labelled a proposed structure
subject to final confirmation.

## What is never presented as confirmed

No event date, venue, speaker, participant, visit, institution, ministry or
sponsor is presented as confirmed. Positive claims equivalent to the following
are absent from the copy (they may appear only inside a negative disclaimer or a
test): registration is open · your place is confirmed · guaranteed meeting ·
confirmed investor/ministry/sponsor/factory visit/delegation · financing
available · partnership secured · automatic matchmaking · approved delegation.

No logos of institutions, banks, ministries, sponsors, companies or ports are
displayed.

## Qualification is not acceptance

The hub's qualification notice states: submitting a request does not confirm
participation; qualification does not guarantee an invitation; an invitation does
not guarantee a meeting with a specific entity; a meeting does not confirm
partnership, financing or contracting; programme details remain subject to final
approval; participant information is not publicly listed. The finance path repeats
that no opportunity is automatically investment-ready, financeable, approved or
open for public subscription.

## Regulated and sensitive elements

IBRIZ/GAAS appears against the mining and ports sector tracks only as a potential
regulated infrastructure concept and is never presented as active project
finance. The mining track and the Sudanese-producers path reiterate that
anonymous assets, minerals, land or projects are never accepted; identity and
documentation come first.

## Privacy boundaries

No participant directory is public; no submitted request is publicly visible; the
qualification form does not publish an organization profile; no automatic
introduction, matching or acceptance is made; visitors are told not to enter
confidential or sensitive information into the public form; additional controlled
exchange may occur later through approved channels. The reception preserves the
existing no-backend `mailto` transport and in-memory state — no analytics,
cookies, storage, uploads, accounts or application numbers. Sensitive identity
fields (passports, national IDs, banking data) are never collected.

## Current verification gaps

All P0/P1/P2 missing-input items remain unresolved and are unchanged by P3. No
Forum date, venue, participant, sponsor, ministry or visit is upgraded to
confirmed until controlled evidence and authorization are supplied.
