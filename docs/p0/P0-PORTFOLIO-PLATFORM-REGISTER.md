## Simplified public statuses (§5)

The visitor-facing card uses only four statuses, each with a last-reviewed date:

- Public Profile Available
- Additional Information Available After Review
- Update Required Before Publication
- Regulated or Sensitive Project

The richer internal `PublicStatus` / `EvidenceState` vocabulary remains in code
for nodes and documentation but is not exposed on the portfolio cards.

## VALURA — Value-Added Agro-Industrial Complex

- Type: integrated agricultural processing and value-addition complex (not an
  economic-zone development model).
- Core value: transform Sudanese crops and raw materials into processed,
  packaged, exportable higher-value products.
- Components: sesame/groundnut/watermelon-seed oil processing; medicinal and
  wild-herb oil extraction; optical + AI-assisted sorting; automated packaging;
  vegetable/fruit sterilization; solar-powered cold storage; hybrid energy,
  waste reduction and by-product reuse; potential RWAFID integration.
- Stage: preliminary industrial and investment blueprint; final site,
  feasibility and ESIA to be completed/updated.
- Locations: potential, under study — Kassala, Gedaref, Gezira. No fixed site;
  not presented as established economic zones.
- Indicative figures (labelled "Preliminary estimates from VALURA Blueprint
  V1.0 — March 2026"): USD 8–12M capital, 50–80 t/day, 50–80 feddans, 250+
  direct / 500+ indirect jobs. IRR, payback and revenue are **not** shown.
- Status: **Public Profile Available — Preliminary Blueprint** · last reviewed
  March 2026.

## RWAFID — Digital Agriculture and Supply-Chain Platform

- Type: offline-first digital agriculture and supply-chain platform.
- Components: farm records; GPS mapping; NDVI/satellite monitoring; early
  warnings; AI + human advisory; input access; financial-service integration;
  buyer/processor access; logistics visibility; offline-first with later sync.

  institutional support and operating partnerships.
- Withheld pending verification: Q1 2026 beta/pilot, 100-farmer pilot,
  5,000-farmer cooperative agreement, Ministry-of-Agriculture letter, completed
  technical design, USD 1.5M funding, farmer-growth projections.
- Status: **Update Required Before Publication** · last reviewed July 2026.

## IBRIZ / GAAS — Digital Gold and Banking Infrastructure Project

- Type: regulated financial-technology and banking-infrastructure project.
- Core value: regulated pathways for gold-related institutional services, trade
  finance, transaction traceability, development-fund transparency,
  agricultural-export finance, bank integration and GAAS for regulated
  institutions.
- Stage: technical and regulatory development project; any banking, payment,
  custody, investment, lending, gold-backed account, trade-finance or
  settlement activity remains subject to licensing, regulatory approval and

- Never presented as: licensed/operating bank, world's-first gold-backed bank,

  central-bank gold holder, guaranteed convertibility, or a service with
  millions of users. No consumer banking CTA.
- Status: **Regulated or Sensitive Project** · last reviewed December 2025.

## Card contract (§6) and Request Project Review (§7)

Each card shows: name, project type, core value, geographic scope, main
components, current stage, limited labelled indicative figures where safe,
partners/capabilities sought, project-file status + last-reviewed date, and a
controlled CTA. The CTA is **Request Project Review / Demander l'examen du
projet / طلب مراجعة المشروع**, routing through the no-backend reception model.
A single concise note states that a review request does not guarantee access,
financing or partnership, that confidential documents are never shared
automatically, and that the specialist team determines the next step. Cards
never publish unverified investment values, expected returns, confidential
sponsors, ownership claims, logos, or readiness labels.
