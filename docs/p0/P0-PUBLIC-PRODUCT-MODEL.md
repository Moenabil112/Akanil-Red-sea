# P0 — Public Product Model

## Approved product definition

Akanil operates an economic and technology ecosystem that structures supply
chains between Morocco, Sudan, Saudi Arabia, Egypt and the Horn of Africa. The
ecosystem connects production and assets with ports, economic zones,
manufacturing, finance, technology and international market access.

## Akanil's public roles

- Founder
- Executive operator
- Ecosystem orchestrator
- Project and supply-chain structuring operator
- Institutional and business-development coordinator
- Technology and evidence governance operator

Akanil is **not** described as a broker or an open marketplace, and never
claims to independently approve investment, financing, licences, standards
compliance or governmental decisions.

## Why the Red Sea (core thesis)

The Red Sea is presented as the economic space where nine layers meet:
production and resources in Sudan and the Horn of Africa; agricultural,
livestock, mining and industrial assets; ports and logistics nodes; economic
and industrial zones; Saudi and Egyptian manufacturing, investment and
distribution; Moroccan manufacturing, processing, standards and quality; trade
finance, insurance and investment institutions; international market-access
routes through Morocco and Red Sea ports; technology, data, traceability and
operational intelligence.

Akanil's distinctive role: structuring the triangular bridge linking East
Africa, the Red Sea and Saudi Arabia, then connecting that bridge with
Morocco's industrial, logistics, financial and export capabilities.

## Value chain (8 steps)

African production and assets → qualification and evidence → aggregation and
primary processing → economic zones and industrial nodes → Red Sea ports and
trade corridors → manufacturing, standards and finance → Morocco as a
transformation and market-access platform → regional and international markets.

Morocco is presented as a transformation platform (industry, processing,
packaging, standards, trade finance, insurance, logistics, distribution,
export preparation, market access) — never merely an end market. Every
market-access reference carries the qualification: *subject to applicable
rules of origin, customs requirements, product standards, regulatory approvals
and the level of transformation performed.*

## Technology and operating intelligence

Capabilities are listed with explicit `CapabilityState` values. The public
window and email reception are `active-public`; specialist review is
`manual-specialist-review`; data rooms and advanced tools are `planned` and
never presented as active. Operating principle: *Technology organizes evidence
and proposes analysis. Authorized specialists review and decide.*

## Specialized review

Requests are reviewed by an authorized multidisciplinary team. Public copy
lists review disciplines (institutional/business development, international
trade, supply chains and logistics, legal and compliance, finance and
investment, agriculture and livestock, mining and industry, technology and
data, project and evidence management) and the routing model: reception →
classification → minimum-information check → relevant specialist review →
evidence-gap identification → recommended next step. Reception ≠ acceptance;
review ≠ endorsement; only authorized humans decide; AI assists but never
approves.
